CHAIRMAN

Founder & Chairman : Sri. K. S. Ravi Kumar

Sri. K.S. Ravi Kumar is an eminent educationist and has been the beacon of success of the various technical Institutions and their growth. He is known for his academic organizing abilities. He was born on 6th of August, 1964  at vemula village in Mahabubnagar District. His primary education was carried in vemula andhigher education at MBC High School, Mahabubnagar. Intermediate and Degree (B.Com) at MVS Degree College in 1984.Later he completed his LLB from Maratwada University,Nanded,Maharastra in 1987. After his LLB he started his practice as an Advocate in Mahabubnagar.

He is also acting as Chairman, Telangana Engineering Colleges Coordination Committee. He is the Secretary/Correspondent of M/s. Comp-Tech. ITI. He has been the beacon and lighthouse for the college, leading it towards success and growth. He is an Academic Advisor for several reputed Institutions. He is  a  Visionary and a  noted Educationist and  devoted  to Academics,  known for his Academic intrest and Organizing abilities. He is the Jt. Secretary of A.P.Private Engineering Colleges Management Association. He is the recipient of Gold Medal, from his Excellency Shree Rameshwar Thakur, Former Governor of A.P. for largset blood donation made from the institution to Red Cross Society every year. He is very generous and encourages many poor merit students for achieving their academic laurels and backbone of their success.

Political Profile: He served as District Youth Congress President-Mahabubnagar(1983 – 1999) and Treasurer of District Congress Committee, Mahabubnagar(1999 – 2000). He Contested from Deverakadra Assembly Constituency during 2009 Elections and secured Highest votes in the District among the PRP candidates.Now, joined Congress Party along with the PRP Cadre in the presence of PCC Chief Bothsa Satyanarayana.
