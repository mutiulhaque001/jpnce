Management

The Promoting body – Vahini Educational Society was formed by enterprising intellectuals of local area and established “Jayaprakash Narayan College of Engineering” with a view to facilitate quality technical education to the youth of rural areas and urbanise them with morals and ethics cherished by Bharat Ratna Loknayak Jayaprakash Narayan. The society was registered under Societies Act with Registrar of Societies, Govt. of A.P., Hyderabad (Regn. No. 401 of 96 dt. 30th January, 1996) Vahini Educational Society was promoted by group of educationists, intellectuals interested in expanding technical education in rural area, Mahabubnagar. Promoters are experienced in the field of technical education as they have already been promoting an Industrial Training Institute in the same region.
